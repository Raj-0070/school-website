About Us

St. Mary's Matriculation Higher Secondary School, located at Arockia Annai Nagar, Poruvalur Moongilthuraipet (Via), Tamil Nadu – 605702, was established in the year 2008.

The school follows the Matriculation Board syllabus and aims to provide holistic education that balances academics, discipline, and character building.
